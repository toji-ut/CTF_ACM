Clever minds in search of treasure,
Adventures await, full of pleasure.
Puzzles and clues are scattered near,
Time to explore, conquer your fear.
Unlock the secrets hidden away,
Reach for the prize, don’t delay.
Every moment counts, stay on track.

Take a breath, and stay on the hack.
Hidden gems in every nook,
Enter the realm, open the book.

Find the paths that twist and wind,
Listen closely, see what you find.
All your skills will now combine,
Gain the knowledge, it’s your time.
